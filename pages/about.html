About

My name is Nathan Bloomfield and this is where I take notes.

I like to think about the (strange|beautiful) interaction between universal algebra and programming. Sometimes I write about that too.

Many of the posts here are literate programs, which is fun. When I’m learning a thing I like to jot down little programs to try it out, and the literate style is good for this. Also it means my blog posts have unit tests. :)
